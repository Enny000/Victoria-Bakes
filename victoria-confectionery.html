Our Story

Made in Bristol, Loved Everywhere

Victoria Confectionery is a small, family-run bakery nestled in the heart of Bristol. Founded over a decade ago with a simple belief: that every celebration deserves a truly exceptional cake.

We pour heart, craft, and only the finest ingredients into every tier, every petal, every brushstroke. From intimate birthday sponges to grand multi-tiered wedding centrepieces — each cake is designed with you, and made for your moment.

Our bespoke process means no two cakes are ever the same. We work closely with you from consultation to delivery, ensuring your vision is brought to life in the most delicious way possible.

How It Works

From Dream to Delivery

A simple, personal process that puts you at the heart of every cake we create.

1

Consultation

Tell us your vision — flavours, theme, occasion, and budget. We listen to every detail.

2

Design

We sketch your bespoke design and share a mood board for your approval before baking begins.

3

Baking

Using the finest ingredients, your cake is baked fresh and decorated to the agreed specification.

4

Delivery

We deliver safely across Bristol and surrounding areas, arriving in perfect condition for your celebration.
